  • Clinical Microbiology

Clinical microbiology laboratory services are essential for the diagnosis and management of infectious diseases and microbial-related conditions. In some cases, hospitals and healthcare facilities may outsource their clinical microbiology testing to external laboratories.

  • Histopathology

Outsourcing histopathology laboratory services is a practice where a hospital or healthcare facility contracts with an external laboratory to perform histopathological testing and provide diagnostic results. Histopathology is a branch of pathology that involves the examination of tissue samples to study the microscopic changes and diagnose diseases.

  • Clinical Haematology

Outsourcing clinical hematology laboratory services is a practice where a hospital or healthcare facility contracts with an external laboratory to perform hematology tests and provide diagnostic results.

  • Cytopathology

Outsourcing cytopathology laboratory services is a practice where a hospital or healthcare facility contracts with an external laboratory to perform cytopathological testing and provide diagnostic results. Cytopathology is a branch of pathology that involves the examination of cells obtained from body fluids or tissues to diagnose diseases.
