General surgery is a surgical specialty that focuses on abdominal contents including the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, large intestine, liver, pancreas, gallbladder, appendix and bile ducts, and often the thyroid gland.

General surgery services encompass a wide range of surgical procedures and treatments aimed at diagnosing, managing, and surgically correcting various medical conditions.

General surgeons are medical professionals who are highly skilled in performing a diverse set of surgical procedures. Here are some aspects of general surgery services for patients:

  • Emergency Surgery:General surgeons are trained to perform emergency surgeries, such as appendectomies, trauma surgeries, and procedures for abdominal emergencies like bowel obstructions or perforations.
  • Inpatient and Outpatient Surgeries: General surgeons perform surgeries in both inpatient and outpatient settings, depending on the complexity of the procedure and the patient's medical condition.

Why Choose This Service

Patients may choose general surgery services for several reasons, as these services offer several key advantages that cater to their specific medical needs and overall well-being.
