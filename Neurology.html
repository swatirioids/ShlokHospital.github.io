Services Of Neurology

Neurology is a branch of medicine dealing with disorders of the nervous system. Neurology deals with the diagnosis and treatment of all categories of conditions and disease involving the central and peripheral nervous systems, including their coverings, blood vessels, and all effector tissue, such as muscle.

Neurology service in a hospital involves the diagnosis, treatment, and management of conditions and disorders affecting the nervous system. Neurologists, who are medical specialists in neurology, provide expertise in the evaluation and care of patients with neurological issues.

Here are some key aspects of neurology service for patients in the hospital:

  • Neurocritical Care:For patients with severe neurological conditions requiring intensive care, neurology services may be part of a multidisciplinary neurocritical care team.
  • Neurogenetics:Some neurologists specialize in neurogenetics, diagnosing and managing inherited neurological disorders with a genetic basis.

Why Choose This Service

By choosing neurology services in our hospital, patients can benefit from specialized care, accurate diagnosis, and access to a wide range of treatment options to address their neurological conditions effectively. Neurologists and the entire neurology team work to improve patients' quality of life, manage symptoms, and support patients and their families throughout their neurological healthcare journey.
